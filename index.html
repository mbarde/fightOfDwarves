Instructions:

  • Press F11 for fullscreen
  • Change player controls: Navigate to player and press enter. Press buttons for displayed actions. Attention: There is no check if buttons are assigned multiple times!
  • Gamepad support: Simply plug in your gamepads. The input will be mapped automatically to the player keys.
  • Dwarves can:
    • Attack
    • Jump
    • Run: left and right
    • Block: If you are attacked while blocking, you wont loose any health but your blocking will stop immediately.
